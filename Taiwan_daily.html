1. Introduction¶

On March 11, 2020, the World Health Organization (WHO) declared novel coronavirus (COVID-19) as a global pandemic. Until now, there are more than 2,000,000 infected cases and more than 150,000 deaths around the world. Although not being part of the WHO, Taiwan reported the first confirmed infected case on January 21, 2020, and started taking a series of precaution to prevent the virus from spreading out. Three months later (April 21, 2020), Taiwan has 422 infected cases, 6 deaths and 189 recovery cases.

In this project, the data analysis of COVID-19 is based on a case study of Taiwan. There are three sections in the project,

  1. A brief overview of the COVID-19 in Taiwan and its trend
  2. Distribution of the COVID-19 confirmed cases based on different categories
  3. The relations between confirmed cases, deaths and recovery cases

Covid19_Taiwan = Table.read_table('嚴重特殊傳染性肺炎 Taiwan CDC 公布病例資訊 - 確診病例.csv')

Covid19_cases = Covid19_Taiwan.group(['確診日', '境外或是本土'])
Covid19_cases_domestic = Covid19_cases.where('境外或是本土', are.equal_to('本土'))
Covid19_cases_imported = Covid19_cases.where('境外或是本土', are.equal_to('境外'))
Covid19_cases_others = Covid19_cases.where('境外或是本土', are.not_equal_to('本土')).where('境外或是本土', are.not_equal_to('境外'))
Covid19_death = Covid19_Taiwan.group('死亡日')

End_date = '20200420'

date = pd.date_range('20200101', End_date)

Covid19_Taiwan_cases = Table().with_columns(
    'Date', date.date,
    'Domestic', np.zeros(len(date.date)),
    'Imported', np.zeros(len(date.date)),
    'Others', np.zeros(len(date.date)),
    'Death', np.zeros(len(date.date))
)

for i in range(Covid19_Taiwan_cases.num_rows):
    Covid19_Taiwan_cases.column('Date')[i] = str(Covid19_Taiwan_cases.column('Date').item(i))
    
def reconstruct(s1, s2, t1, t2):
    for i in range(t1.num_rows):
        for j in range(t2.num_rows):
            if t1.column('Date').item(i) == t2.column(s1).item(j):
                t1.column(s2)[i] = t2.column('count').item(j)
    return t1
                
Covid19_Taiwan_cases = reconstruct('確診日', 'Domestic', Covid19_Taiwan_cases, Covid19_cases_domestic)
Covid19_Taiwan_cases = reconstruct('確診日', 'Imported', Covid19_Taiwan_cases, Covid19_cases_imported)
Covid19_Taiwan_cases = reconstruct('確診日', 'Others', Covid19_Taiwan_cases, Covid19_cases_others)      
Covid19_Taiwan_cases = reconstruct('死亡日', 'Death', Covid19_Taiwan_cases, Covid19_death)

In this section, the daily confirmed cases of COVID-19 and deaths in Taiwan are presented. All the graphs below indicate the date on the x-axis.

The daily confirmed cases have been separated into three different categories based on the infected sources, including domestic, imported and others. 5. Conclusions¶

In this project, the situation of COVID-19 in Taiwan has been examined in different aspects, including the current trend, distribution of confirmed cases and relations between variables. The conclusions are as follows:

  1. The amount of confirmed cases is still increasing but the increasing rate is relatively slow compared with one month before.
  2. The total of deaths is less than 1.5% of the total confirmed cases.
  3. More than 80% of the confirmed cases are imported cases and most of the confirmed cases are in the north part of Taiwan, especially the capital, Taipei City.
  4. Currently, the amount of recovery cases is increasing stably and faster than the number of confirmed cases and deaths.
